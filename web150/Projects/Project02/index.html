Nevada Vegas

Better Than You Think

Nature

While nature isn't the first thing we think of when going to a desert in seemingly the middle of nowhere, Nevada and states nearby are home to some of the most beautiful parks in the nation. Nevada can not only serve as a base for adventuring the South-West but as a place its-self to explore. Roam the desert in the heat of the South-West the same as the cowboys. Maybe you'll find your golden
location out there.

Entertainment

We all know Vegas for the gambling and adult shows, but the other lesser-explored side deserve a chance to shine. World-class comedic acts, nail-biting performances, and graceful art are all in-store at the right place. But, the fun doesn't stop there. One moment you can be taking a picture by a model statue of liberty, and then in a canoe in France. The adventures possible in this
mini-world are endless!

Dining

In Vegas, the food industry is survival of the fittest, be the best or get out. The turnover rate is high which some see as a problem. But instead, this turns into a playground of food experimentation. The bedrock restaurants have been tested by time and competition to be the best. The new restaurants are trying to establish a name for themselves. The variety of fine dining, fast food, and something in-between means options will never run out.
