MGM Grand

MGM Grand is an entertainment palace that isn't only limited to the boxing ring. There are a countless number of high-brow shows on a weekly basis whether it be art, comedy, or plays. You can live a luxurious life in the hotel, such as watching sports in their Level Up lounge or playing golf with family at the TopGolf Range. They also have a vast and awe-inspiring design that you could venture around for hours! Come check it out and spend some hours roaming the hotel.

Hours: 24/7

Location: 3799 S Las Vegas Blvd

Price: $$$

Circus Circus

Circus Circus is a playground for all ages! They stay true to the theme of the circus by keeping all the activities focused on laughing, fun, and entertainment! Free circus acts, theme parks, and game rooms all provide an experience that won't be forgotten. The Midway is a classic arcade game area, the Adventure Dome is a huge theme park with over 20 rides, and the acts of clowns or magicians will keep that magical feeling going for hours on end!

Hours: 10AM - 9PM

Location: 2880 S Las Vegas Blvd

Price: $$
