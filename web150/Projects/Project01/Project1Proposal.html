Project 01 Proposal

Concept Statement

The subject of my website is going to be about the different recovery methods from exercise and the history of said methods.
My audience is going to be athletes and those who exercise. This is most likely going to fall in the 15-25 age range.
I plan to cover 3 major forms of recovery; ice/heat therapy, stretching, and sleep. The content will be how its performed and the background/origins of it.

Interface Design

The interface of the website will be compact with examples of the information I am presenting. Typing paragraphs won't really help users understand what the form of recovery is or how it is performed. The majority of the text will the science behind it or history.
I want to look at the page and feel like I can trust what is being said, so a message that I am giving legitimate advise.
The message I want to communicate is that of a level of professionalism; well placed appropriate images, videos, and tools to help users understand the concepts being presented.
